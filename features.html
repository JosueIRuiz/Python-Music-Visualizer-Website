Suite Capabilities

Our centralized UI Hub allows users to launch several distinct types of audio visualizers.

Live Microphone Tools

  • Main Visualizer: Circular waveform with true-pitch note detection.
  • Spectrogram: Scrolling waterfall display of frequencies.
  • Color / Avg Frequency: Bar graphs mapping frequencies to a dynamic color space.
  • Live Waveform: Classic oscilloscope-style display.

File Video Exporters

  • Custom Streaming Playback Controller
  • Circular Visualizer (Pitch & No Pitch modes)
  • Bar Graph Visualizer (Pitch & No Pitch modes)
  • Classic Waveform Exporter

Global Color Themes

Users can completely change the aesthetic of their visualizations by selecting one of our 8 custom mathematical color mapping themes: Rainbow, Ocean, Fire, Matrix, Sunset, Cyberpunk, Forest, and Monochrome.
